Drive is a decentralized infrastructure management tool that enables users to deploy and manage blockchain nodes, services, and protocols on their own systemsâ€”whether local machines, personal servers, or their own internet infrastructure.

When combined with other independent users running Drive, it creates a synchronized super-mesh of services, protocols, and chains, forming a distributed infrastructure network managed entirely by independent users. Drive empowers individuals to participate in and contribute to a decentralized ecosystem while maintaining full control over their infrastructure.
